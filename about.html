About ZenCloud

Our journey to becoming India's most trusted Minecraft hosting provider, driven by passion and innovation.

Our Story

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders in Minecraft hosting.

The Beginning

Founded in 2025 by a group of Minecraft enthusiasts frustrated with the lack of quality hosting options in India. We started with just two servers in a Mumbai data center, serving our local gaming community.

1

First Major Upgrade

In 2025 June, we invested in our first enterprise-grade hardware and expanded to multiple locations across India. Our player base grew to 1,00+ active users, validating our commitment to quality hosting.

2

ZenCloud 2.0

2025 July marked our complete infrastructure overhaul with AMD Ryzen 9 processors, NVMe storage, and 10Gbps DDoS protection. We launched our new control panel with one-click modpack installations.

3

Today & Beyond

With 10,000+ happy players and 500+ active servers, we continue to innovate. Our roadmap includes expanding to Southeast Asia, introducing cloud-based world backups, and developing custom hosting solutions for large networks.

4

Our Mission & Values

What drives us to deliver exceptional Minecraft hosting experiences.

Performance First

We believe lag-free gameplay is non-negotiable. That's why we invest in cutting-edge hardware and optimize every layer of our infrastructure for maximum Minecraft performance.

Community Focused

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. We actively engage with our community, listen to feedback, and develop features that enhance their Minecraft experience.

Reliability Matters

We maintain a 99.9% uptime guarantee through redundant systems, proactive monitoring, and enterprise-grade DDoS protection to keep your server online.
