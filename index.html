Getting started is easy! Simply choose a plan that suits your needs, complete the checkout process, and your Minecraft server will be set up instantly. You'll receive login details to your server control panel where you can manage your server, install mods, and invite players.

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time! Your server data will be automatically transferred to your new plan with minimal downtime. Simply go to your account dashboard and select the upgrade option.
